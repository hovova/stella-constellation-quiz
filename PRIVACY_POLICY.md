# Privacy Policy for Stella

**Effective date:** 3 June 2026

Stella is a constellation quiz and educational astronomy game developed by **Mriya Interactive**.

This Privacy Policy explains how Stella handles information when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

Stella does not require users to create an account, log in, or provide personal information directly to use the app.

The app stores gameplay progress locally on your device, including information such as:

* completed levels
* XP and progress
* achievements
* selected language
* selected avatar and avatar frame
* sound and music preferences
* premium or no-ads status

This information is stored on your device and is used only to provide the app’s gameplay features.

## 2. Advertising

Stella may show advertisements to users who have not purchased the No Ads upgrade or Premium upgrade.

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect or process certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app activity, approximate location, and technical information, depending on your device settings and Google’s advertising policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Users who purchase No Ads or Premium will not see ads in Stella.

## 3. In-App Purchases

Stella may offer in-app purchases, including:

* No Ads
* Premium

Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. Stella does not collect or store your payment card details. Payment processing is handled by Google Play according to Google’s own policies.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

Stella is designed as an educational quiz game about constellations and astronomy. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that we can review and address the issue.

## 5. Data Sharing

Stella does not sell your personal information.

Some third-party services used by the app, such as Google AdMob and Google Play Billing, may process data as necessary to provide advertising, purchase, fraud prevention, analytics, and platform services.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

Gameplay progress and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Third-party services used by the app may process data according to their own privacy and security practices.

## 7. User Choices

You can control certain data and app behaviour by:

* disabling music or sound effects in the app
* purchasing No Ads or Premium to remove advertising
* clearing app data from your Android device settings
* uninstalling the app

Clearing app data or uninstalling the app may delete locally stored progress.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted by updating this file or the app’s privacy policy page.
